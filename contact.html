Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping is available for 1-2 business days.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship worldwide. International delivery typically takes 7-14 business days.

What is your return policy?

We offer 30-day returns for unused items in original packaging. Return shipping is free.

Do you have physical stores?

Currently we operate online only, but we're planning to open flagship stores in major cities soon.

Can I modify my order after placing it?

Order modifications are possible within 1 hour of placement. Contact us immediately for changes.

Do you offer wholesale pricing?

Yes, we offer special pricing for retailers and bulk orders. Please use the wholesale inquiry form.
